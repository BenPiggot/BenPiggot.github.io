About Me

Hi, my name is Ben Piggot and I am web developer passionate about working on projects that visualize information in new and powerful ways. I originally trained as a professional historian because I have a passion for gathering, analyzing, and conveying information. These are the same passions that motivate me today in my current career. I am primarily a front-end developer who works in a variety of JavaScript frameworks and libraries. I also have experience working with Ruby/Rails, and SQL (MySQL, Postgres), and R.
